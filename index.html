About us | Sobre nosotros

We offer treatment for a wide range of conditions, including respiratory problems such as fibrosis and COPD.

Our approach is based on therapeutic exercise, manual therapy and patient information. Other treatments such as dry needling, electrotherapy or ultrasound may be added depending on the individual treatment plan.

Our treatments are driven by SMART objectives whenever possible. This way our patients know exactly what they can expect from us and they can see and measure, if we have met our goals.

During the treatment the relationship with our patients is similar to the one a trainer has with his athlete. It is the patient who puts in all the work. Any progress or success is the patient's own merit. We just do the coaching and anything that is required to reach goals established and agreed upon.
